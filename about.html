About Golden Gual

Golden Gual is a brilliant iconic hotel located near Negombo beach. Our outdoor spaces are abundant in the natural beauty of the Negombo beach , providing a one-of-a-kind and fascinating backdrop for a variety of events. Whether you're planning a wedding, a corporate event, or any other special occasion. All halls are stylishly decorated and equipped with every luxury you would want for an enjoyable experience.

The daytime filled with sunshine calls for outdoor events. At night it’s time for some good entertainment on the beautiful shores of the Indian Ocean.With each passing year, we have grown not just in size, but also in experience and competence, making us the ideal choice for guests wanting exceptional hospitality and a natural beauty backdrop.
